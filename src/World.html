A not at all accurate depiction of the solar system.
Move around with WASD and drag across the screen to use the regular camera.
To use the orbital camera, press esc and drag, it will start an orbital camera centered at the sun.
There are 20 primary objects, 8 planets, 1 sun, 1 satellite, 2 rings, and 8 torus to depict the orbits.
There is 3 light sources, ambient light, point light (the sun), and a green directional light above.
For 3 extra features, I have shadows, billboards, and picking. Press esc and click on an object to get a label for it.
The earth is a rendered object from an online source.
